Privacy Policy

Your data.
Your business.

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Applies to imajpg.vercel.app

The short version: imajpg does not collect, store, or transmit your images or personal data. All image processing happens entirely within your browser. We have no access to your files.

1 Who We Are

Imajpg is a free, browser-based image toolkit operated by Atom_Wrek, accessible at imajpg.vercel.app. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at atom-wrek@proton.me.

2 Information We Do Not Collect

Imajpg does not collect, process, or store any of the following:

  • The images or files you upload or process using our tools
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifying information
  • Your account details (we have no accounts or login system)
  • Your location or IP address beyond what your browser or hosting provider may log at the network level

All image conversion, compression, and resizing operations are performed locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. No file data is ever transmitted to any server.

3 Cookies and Tracking

Imajpg itself does not use cookies or any first-party tracking technology. However, third-party services used on this site may set cookies on your device.

Specifically, we display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to imajpg and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

4 Third-Party Services

Imajpg uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Vercel — our hosting provider. Vercel may collect standard server access logs (IP addresses, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal hosting operations. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — we load fonts from Google's servers. This involves your browser making a request to Google, which may log your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — we display advertisements. Google may use cookies to personalise ads shown to you. See Google's Advertising Policy.

5 Your Image Files

To be explicitly clear: the files you process using imajpg are handled entirely within your own browser. When you select or drop an image file, it is read by your browser's local file API. When you convert, compress, or resize it, those operations are performed by JavaScript running in your browser tab using the HTML5 Canvas API.

At no point does your image file leave your device or get transmitted over the internet to any server — including ours.

6 Children's Privacy

Imajpg does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The site does not require registration or any personal information to use. If you believe a child has somehow provided personal information through this site, please contact us at atom-wrek@proton.me and we will address it promptly.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, particularly as new tools or third-party services are added to the site. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of imajpg after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8 Your Rights — GDPR & CCPA

Depending on where you are located, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data under applicable law, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for residents of California.

Because imajpg does not collect or store any personal data from its users, there is in practice no data held on you that can be accessed, corrected, or deleted. However, if you have any concern or wish to make a data-related request, you are welcome to contact us at atom-wrek@proton.me and we will respond within 48 hours.

For data processed by third-party services such as Google (AdSense, Fonts) or Vercel, please refer directly to their respective privacy policies linked in Section 4.

9 Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how imajpg handles data, you can reach us at atom-wrek@proton.me. We will respond within 48 hours.
