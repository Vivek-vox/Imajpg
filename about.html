About Imajpg

Built to keep
things simple.

imajpg is a free, browser-based image toolkit — convert, resize, and compress images instantly without uploading anything to a server. Built as the first project under Atom_Wrek, it's designed to be fast, private, and straightforward. More tools are on the way.

100% Private

Your images never leave your device. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Instant Processing

No waiting for uploads or server responses. Results are ready in seconds.

Always Free

No signup, no paywalls, no limits. Every tool on imajpg is completely free to use.

No Dependencies

No frameworks, no external libraries. Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using browser APIs.

The Project

What is Atom_Wrek?

Atom_Wrek is an independent development initiative focused on building practical, no-nonsense tools for everyday use. imajpg is its first project — a starting point, not the full picture. More tools across different categories are planned for the future.

The goal is simple: build things that actually work, keep them free, and keep them private. No bloat, no subscriptions, no data harvesting.

Why imajpg?

Most image tools online either require an account, upload your files to a remote server, or put the most useful features behind a paywall. imajpg was built to fix all three of those problems at once.

Everything runs in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your files are processed locally and never transmitted anywhere. What you do with your images is your business.

Current Tools

imajpg currently offers image conversion between JPG, PNG, WEBP, and BMP formats, image compression with adjustable quality control, and image resizing with automatic aspect ratio support. Batch processing and bulk download are supported across all tools.
